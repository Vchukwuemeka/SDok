About us

We're a team of 7 changing Africa's talent economy.

6 talented Africans who got tired of moving from one freelance platform to the other, waiting for the next big gig to drop.

This led us to building a solution that provides African freelancers the platform to showcase their skills, connect with clients and participate in the global digital economy

We aim to reduce the friction that exists in the hiring process of Africans, making the process relatively easier for businesses and individuals to hire from Africa.

Our Ultimate goal is to enable African talents not only to succeed but thrive on a global scale.

So, whether you're an OG who has been in the freelance game or a newbie finding it difficult to sell your skills/services to those who need them. You've come to end of your search!
